## What good web design in Kensington looks like

Strong local websites tend to share the same handful of qualities, whether you sell flat whites near Gloucester Road or fit kitchens for Notting Hill townhouses. Our approach to [website design](/services/website-design) is built around them:

- **Fast on mobile:** pages that load in a second or two, even on patchy signal near South Kensington station.
- **One clear action:** a phone number, booking link or enquiry form that is never more than a tap away.
- **Trust at a glance:** real photos, honest pricing or ranges, and reviews that reassure a cautious, well-travelled audience.
- **Easy to read:** generous spacing and plain language, not clever copy nobody scans.
- **Yours to update:** a simple admin panel so you can change opening hours or add an offer without ringing a developer.
