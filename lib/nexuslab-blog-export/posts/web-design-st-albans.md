## What makes a website convert, not just look nice

Plenty of St Albans businesses have a website that looks fine and still generates almost nothing. The gap between a pretty site and a profitable one comes down to a handful of things done well.

- A headline that says exactly what you do and where — "Emergency dentist in St Albans", not "Welcome to our website".
- Fast loading on mobile, where most local searches now happen.
- One obvious next step on every page: call, book or send an enquiry.
- Real trust signals — genuine photos, Google reviews and your actual address, not stock images.
- Copy that speaks to the customer's problem instead of listing your features.
- The local SEO groundwork that helps you show up when someone searches "near me".

> A beautiful website that doesn't bring in enquiries is just an expensive business card.
