## What web design in Liverpool really needs to do

A good-looking website that nobody can find is a poster in a locked room. Before it wins you anything, your site has to do three plain jobs: load quickly, make it obvious what you do and where you do it, and make getting in touch effortless. People decide fast — land them on a cluttered homepage, or a phone number they have to pinch and zoom to read, and they've judged your whole business before reading a word. Liverpool is a competitive place to trade. Independents on Smithdown Road and Lark Lane chase the same custom, and the trades quote against half a dozen others for the same job. The businesses that win online are rarely the flashiest — they're the ones with the clearest, fastest, most trustworthy site, where a visitor knows within seconds they're in the right place.
