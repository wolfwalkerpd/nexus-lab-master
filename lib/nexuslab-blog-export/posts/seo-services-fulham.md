## The Google map pack: how Fulham customers find you

The map pack — sometimes called the local pack — is the little map with three business listings that appears at the top of most local searches. For "near me" and service-plus-place searches, think "coffee Fulham Broadway" or "orthodontist Parsons Green", it sits above the ordinary blue links and takes the lion's share of the taps. Getting into those three spots is often worth more than ranking first in the normal results.

Crucially, those listings are powered by your Google Business Profile, not your website directly. Google decides who to feature using three things: relevance, distance and prominence — how complete and accurate your profile is, how close you are to the person searching, and how many recent, genuine reviews and mentions you have around the web.

> For most Fulham searches, the three businesses in the map pack take the majority of the clicks. Being fourth is much the same as being invisible.

## What good SEO services in Fulham look like

There's no single magic switch, but there is a reliable list of things that move the needle. Get these right and you make it as easy as possible for Google to trust you and show you:

- A complete, accurate Google Business Profile — correct categories, opening hours, services and real photos of your Fulham premises, not stock images.
- A consistent business name, address and phone number everywhere online, so Google is confident you're a genuine SW6 business.
- A steady trickle of honest reviews, with a short reply to each one. This is the single biggest lever most local businesses leave untouched.
- Pages written around what people actually type — "emergency electrician Fulham" rather than a vague "our services".
- Local relevance signals: being listed on Fulham directories and community pages, and mentioned by nearby businesses you work alongside.
- A fast, mobile-first website, because most local searches here happen on a phone, often outdoors on patchy signal.
