## Why a good-looking site isn't enough in Hackney

Hackney is one of the most competitive patches in London for small businesses. A single stretch of Chatsworth Road or Broadway Market can hold a dozen independents fighting for the same customers, and most of those customers are searching on their phones — walking down Kingsland High Street, sitting in a café in London Fields, or checking reviews before they leave the house.

That changes what your website has to do. It isn't a digital brochure anymore. It's the moment someone decides whether you're worth the trip. If your site loads slowly, hides your opening hours, or looks like it was thrown together years ago, people bounce back to Google and pick the next result — often a competitor who has done nothing more than make things easy to find.

Good design fixes that quietly. Clear headings, fast loading on mobile, an obvious way to get in touch, and honest photos of your actual place. None of it is flashy. All of it earns trust in the few seconds you get.
