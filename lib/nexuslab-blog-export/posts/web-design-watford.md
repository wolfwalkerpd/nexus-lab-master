## What a website should actually do for your business

Forget the jargon. A website earns its keep when it does a handful of simple things well:

- Loads in a second or two on a phone, because most local searches happen on mobile
- Makes it obvious within seconds what you do and which Watford areas you cover
- Keeps your phone number, opening hours and an enquiry or booking form within easy reach
- Shows up in Google when someone nearby searches for your service
- Builds enough trust — real photos, clear pricing, a clear next step — that people choose you over a stranger

> A good website is not the one that wins design awards. It is the one that turns a Google search in Watford into a phone call for you.
