## What SEO services in Ealing actually involve

Good [local SEO](/services/seo) isn't one magic trick. It's a handful of unglamorous things done consistently. Here's what actually moves the needle for a business in Ealing:

Done together, these signals tell Google you're a real, active business that people in Ealing rely on.

- **Google Business Profile:** a complete, accurate listing with the right categories, opening hours, photos and service areas — this is what feeds the map pack.
- **Reviews:** a steady stream of genuine Google reviews, and thoughtful replies to them. Nothing builds local trust faster.
- **On-page basics:** clear page titles, headings and copy that name what you do and where — Ealing and the neighbourhoods you cover.
- **Local content:** pages that answer the real questions your customers ask, written for people first and search engines second.
- **Technical health:** a site that loads fast on mobile, works on every screen and has no broken pages dragging you down.
- **Citations and links:** consistent business details across directories, plus mentions from local and industry websites.

## Winning the Google map pack

The map pack is the single biggest prize in local search. It sits above almost everything else and comes with a map, star ratings and a tap-to-call button, so it collects the clicks. Only three businesses make the cut. Google decides mainly on three things: how relevant you are to the search, how close you are to the searcher, and how prominent you look — reviews, links and an active profile all feed that last one.

Proximity is the one lever you can't pull. That's why the businesses that win consistently are the ones that get the controllable parts right: a polished profile, a regular flow of reviews and a website that backs the whole thing up.
