## The website features that win new patients

The practices that win new patients online all get the same handful of things right. None of it is flashy — it is about removing friction and building trust in the moments that matter.

Get these right and your site does the quiet, consistent selling a busy practice needs. It is exactly what we build into every [dental website](/services/website-design) we design.

- Online booking that works at 10pm — most patients search outside working hours, so a simple request-an-appointment form captures them before the urge passes.
- A clear new-patient offer, such as a first-visit check-up or a free Invisalign consultation, which gives hesitant patients a reason to choose you today.
- Real Google reviews on the page — Wimbledon patients trust other locals, so put that social proof where they will see it first.
- Honest trust signals: GDC registration, team photos and qualifications, and any memberships that reassure private patients spending real money.
- Guide prices for treatments like Invisalign, implants and whitening, which filter out time-wasters and build confidence before the first call.
- Fast, mobile-first pages with tap-to-call and a map, because most local dental searches happen on a phone that wants an answer in one thumb move.
