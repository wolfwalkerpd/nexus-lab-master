## What actually makes a website convert

A website that looks lovely but sits quietly is just an expensive brochure. The sites that bring in enquiries tend to share a handful of unglamorous habits.

None of this is flashy. It is the difference between a site that quietly costs you money and one that earns its keep. Our approach to [web design](/services/website-design) is built entirely around these fundamentals.

- A clear offer in the first few seconds — who you help, where, and what to do next.
- Fast loading on mobile, because that is where most Fulham customers will find you.
- Your phone number, booking link or enquiry form always within a thumb's reach.
- Real local proof: photos of your work, your team and your actual premises, not stock images.
- Plain, confident copy that answers the questions people ask before they ring.
