## What makes a website work for a local Ealing business

A website that brings in enquiries isn't about clever animations or a huge photo library. For a local business it comes down to a handful of fundamentals:

Get those right and everything else — smart [website design](/services/website-design), tidy branding, extra pages — turns a solid foundation into a genuinely strong one.

- Speed on mobile. Most local searches happen on a phone, often on patchy signal on the Central or Elizabeth line — your pages need to appear in a second or two, not four.
- A clear local message. "Same-week appointments near Ealing Broadway" beats a clever tagline every time.
- One obvious action. Call, book or request a quote — repeated, and impossible to miss.
- Trust signals. Real photos of your work, genuine reviews and a proper W5, W13 or W7 address reassure a first-time visitor.
- Answers to real questions. Opening hours, parking near the Broadway, prices and the areas you cover across the borough.
