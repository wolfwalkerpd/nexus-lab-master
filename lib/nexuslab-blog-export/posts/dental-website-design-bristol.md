Bristol is not one market, it is a dozen small ones stitched together. A website that speaks to a specific neighbourhood, and answers the practical questions, wins the click.

## The features that turn Bristol searchers into patients

When we run a free teardown of a dental site, the same handful of things decide whether a visitor books or bounces. Get these right and everything else gets easier:

- **Online booking that works on a phone.** Most dental searches in Bristol happen on mobile, often out of hours. A tap-to-book or request-appointment form beats a phone number nobody rings at 11pm.
- **A clear new-patient offer.** A simple 'new patient exam and hygiene' price, or a free consultation, gives a nervous first-timer a reason to choose you today.
- **Reviews front and centre.** Real Google reviews near the top reassure a stranger far more than any amount of polished copy.
- **Fast, clean mobile pages.** If your site takes more than a few seconds to load on 4G near Temple Meads, people leave before they read a word.
- **The practical details, up top.** Address, parking, nearest bus routes, opening hours, and whether you take nervous patients or children.

## Show you can be trusted before they call

Choosing a dentist is personal. People are handing you their health, their children and, let's be honest, their anxiety. Your website has seconds to feel safe and real. Stock photos of strangers in white coats do the opposite, they whisper 'we could be anyone'.

Show the actual team and the actual reception. Name your dentists and their GDC registration. Put a handful of genuine before-and-after photos where it makes sense. Explain what happens at a first visit so a nervous patient knows exactly what to expect. Every one of these is a small reason to trust you, and trust is what gets the phone to ring.

> Patients don't book the practice with the fanciest website. They book the one that feels like real people who will look after them.
