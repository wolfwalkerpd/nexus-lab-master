## Signs your Guildford website is due a redesign

You do not need to be a designer to spot a site that has aged. Your customers feel it before you do — they land on the page, something does not sit right, and they tap straight back to Google to try the next name down. Most local searches in Surrey now happen on a phone, often on the move between the Friary and the station, so a layout that only really works on a desktop is quietly losing you enquiries every week.

Recognise more than two of the signs below and a redesign is worth taking seriously.

- It loads slowly, especially on mobile data around the town centre or on the train into Waterloo.
- Text is small and buttons are fiddly to tap on a phone.
- The design looks a decade behind the businesses you compete with.
- You cannot update it yourself, so it never really changes.
- It does not appear when people search for your service in Guildford.
- Enquiries have quietly dried up and you cannot put your finger on why.

## Why a website redesign in Guildford pays off
